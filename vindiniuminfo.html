Vindinium

What is Vindinium?

Vindinium is a game where you code your bot to take mines and fight other players inorder to earn gold. The game works in turns each turn you get 1 movement which can be used to walk towards mines(which are used to get gold) walk to tavards which resttore health or attack other players. When you kill an enemy you gain all of their claimed mines and the player with the most gold once all of the turns are up wins the game
